Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture into bottom of the pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla, mixing well.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in sour cream and flour.
  5. Pour batter over crust and bake for 60-70 minutes.
  6. Turn off the oven and let cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
